Assistask
Manager 2

Type your task the way you'd say it out loud. Assistask figures out the priority and due date automatically — no forms, no second pass, no friction.

Download Free → See How It Works
Features
⚡
Natural language capture
Type the way you think. Phrases like "next tuesday," "asap," or "end of month" are parsed automatically into real due dates.
🎯
Auto-priority detection
Add "Important!" or "urgent" to your task text and it's flagged P1. No separate dropdowns or forms — one step, done.
📌
Always on top
Summon the window over any app. Perfect for jotting down tasks mid-meeting without losing focus on what's on screen.
🌍
2 custom timezones
Set two extra clocks for teammates in different cities. Always know what time it is for them, right in the status bar.
📋
Quick summary
Get a glanceable digest of what's pending, what's overdue, and what's done. No dashboards, no clutter.
💸
Free and open source
No trial, no paywall, no subscription — ever. Just download it, use it, and keep it. No catch.
